DISPOSAL OF REAL ESTATE

Act 215 of 1861

AN ACT to authorize boards of health to dispose of real estate.


History: 1861, Act 215, Eff. June 15, 1861




The People of the State of Michigan enact:


327.301 Sale of real estate; fee in board; cemetery ground, sale by court order.

Sec. 1.

   That any board of health of this state may sell and convey any real estate, the fee of which is vested in them: Provided, That no real estate shall be sold by virtue of this act which is or has been in actual use as a cemetery or burial ground, unless the same shall be sold by an order of the circuit court upon the petition of the board of health of the township in which the burial ground is situated.
